Homeland for Special Childrens

Aims and Objective

  • 1.Project aims to develop a care center for Mentally Challanged Childrens with a goal,so that the children who do not have guardian or relative
  • to take care of them can have,"Home To Live In".
  • 2.Those childrens who have parents for them Fullday and Temporary facilities would be available.
  • 3.Special education,Training and Residential facilities to children can be available.
  • 4.Rehabilitation activities for Meantally Challanged Children can be conducted.
  • Events for Parents and Childrens would be conducted.
    • Online admission would be available.

Significance

  • 1.Admission types such as Fullday,Residential and temporary can be available.
  • 2.Childrens whould undergo vocational training to learn and lead decent life.
  • 3.To create awareness among the members of society about the formidable challanges faced by mentally challaned and to encourage their participation in all fields.
