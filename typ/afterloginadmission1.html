Admission

Admission Criteria

  • Mentally Challenged Children of both sexes are eligible for admission in day school.
  • The age should be between 06 to 20 years.
  • I.Q of the child should be above 20.
  • Comprehensive medical, psychological & Education assessment is done prior to admission
  • The child should be fit for education and training duly assessed by the team of experts at GRIID.
  • Preferably the child should be toilet trained and does not need custodial care.

Steps followed for Admission :-

  • First of all a child is assessed in the Diagnostic Clinic.
  • Medical Assessment, Speech & Language Assessment & Physio Occupational Therapy Assessment are got carried out. reports are collected and a conclusion is drawn. The parents of the child are guided/counselled accordingly. If a child is found eligible, he is given admission. If seats are not vacant he/she is kept in the waiting list. The child who is not found eligible and parents of the child are willing to opt for the Home Training Programme, are given Home Training. The child who is not found eligible his parents are guided to other Institutions for admission.

Residential Setting:-

  • Minimum 10years and maximum 20years.

Other Condition for Admission under Residential Setting:-

  1. The Child must be toilet trained ( For Residential Setting only)
  2. The Child must not be having epileptic seizures.( For Residential Setting only)
  3. The Child must not be having Hyperkinesis.( For Residential Setting only)
